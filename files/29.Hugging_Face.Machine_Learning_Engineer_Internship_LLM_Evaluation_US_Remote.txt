Here at Hugging Face, we’re on a journey to advance good Machine Learning and make it more accessible. Along the way, we contribute to the development of technology for the better. We have built the fastest-growing, open-source, library of pre-trained models in the world. With more than 500 K+ models and 250 K+ stars on Git Hub, over 15.000 companies are using H F technology in production, including leading A I organizations such as Google, Elastic, Salesforce, Algolia, and Grammarly. About the Role Evaluation is an incredibly important component of the latest wave of L L M democratization. Producing fair and reproducible results for newly released models enables the community to compare them to one another and to select the one that best fits their needs. The goal of this internship will be to contribute to the increasingly popular “ Open L L M Leaderboard” in order to promote its growth and enable trustworthy evaluation for the ecosystem. Topics encompass community interactions & discussions, implementing fair and interesting ways to display model results, and contributing to future iterations of the leaderboard. Join and help in evaluating M L!
