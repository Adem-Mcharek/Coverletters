Salesforce Inc. seeks Intern - Tax Data Analyst in San Francisco, C A or Seattle, W A: This role has a direct impact to the U S income tax compliance function in two key areas: The team is seeking a tax data analyst intern to collect, organize and interpret tax return data to assist review, provide management insights, address questions and generate predictive risk assessments The team also needs a proposal of a future state cloud-based filing system What You’ll do: Design, build, and maintain interactive dashboards in Tableau and Tableau C R M, ensuring efficient performance and handling of row-level security. Build dashboard tutorials, document process flows, and establish standards and requirements for various use cases Wrangle and analyze large datasets using Salesforce, S Q L/ Snowflake, Tableau Prep, or Alteryx, etc, to identify and handle risks in various processes. Develop and maintain robust data validation processes to ensure data accuracy across different dashboards. Conduct an assessment of current tax document storage model to highlight pain points and gaps, validate findings with internal teams and propose a future state storage system Minimum Qualifications: Currently enrolled full-time in college and pursuing a Business ( Accounting), Data Science, Statistics, Economics or Computer science degree (with a focus in business analytics or data science is desired) Graduation date between December 2024 - August 2025 Experience telling stories with data and familiarity with Tableau visualization tool Proficent in Excel Experience applying your analytics skills to projects which have had impact on business strategy and performance Interest in unlocking new opportunities for growth by discovering insights, automate processes and predictive modeling. Ability to craft analysis into well-written content Preferred Qualifications: Working knowledge of basic statistical concepts (e.g. regressions, A/ B tests, clustering, probability) and how to apply them to practical questions Basic knowledge of Tax industry Accommodations If you require assistance due to a disability applying for open positions please submit a request via this Accommodations Request Form.
