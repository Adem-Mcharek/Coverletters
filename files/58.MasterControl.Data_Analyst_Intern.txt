 S U M M A R Y   
 Interns on the internal  Data/ Analytics team at  Master Control are responsible for maintaining and developing new insights based on organizational data. You will be working directly with senior analysts to conceptualize, design, and produce visually compelling business reporting solutions through dashboards, reports, and datasets using  Domo, outside applications and queries/scripts.  This role requires an ability to see problems in process and recommend solutions that will be beneficial to the end user and to producing high integrity data.  You should have experience analyzing large data sets, enjoy diving deep into data analysis, and discovering root causes.  Members of the data and F P& A teams should be effective communicators capable of driving solutions and communicating insights to non-technical audiences.
