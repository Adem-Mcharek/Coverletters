Job Description: Summer 2024 Internship - Partnerships & Alliances ( E M E A)
 Application Deadline: February 29, 2024
 We encourage you to submit your application as soon as you can as internship applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.
 Internship Dates: July 1, 2024 - September 6, 2024 ( You must be available to work during this period)
 About Us:
 Dow Jones is a global provider of news and business information, delivering content to consumers and organizations around the world across multiple formats, including print, digital, mobile and live events. Dow Jones has produced unrivaled quality content for more than 130 years and today has one of the world’s largest news-gathering operations globally. It is home to leading publications and products including the flagship Wall Street Journal, America’s largest newspaper by paid circulation; Barron’s, Market Watch, Mansion Global, Financial News, Investor’s Business Daily, Factiva, Dow Jones Risk & Compliance, Dow Jones Newswires, O P I S and Chemical Market Analytics. Dow Jones is a division of News Corp ( Nasdaq: N W S, N W S; A S X: N W S, N W S L V).
 About the Role:
 The Dow Jones Partners & Alliances group in the News, Alerts, and Data team is looking for an undergraduate student to join our team for Summer 2024. You’ll gain hands-on experience and work alongside business stakeholders. You are eager to dive in and are not afraid to ask questions, take risks, and learn new things. We are looking for someone with the desire to contribute to our technology driven work environment. You must also work well with diverse teams and be able to take on initiatives and lead projects.
 This role is hybrid, based in our London office.
 You Will:
 Help review our team overlap and help to identify opportunities between Partners, Licensing, Corporate Partners, and R& C teams
 Conduct data collection, analysis, and reporting
 Identify new and emerging firms/publishers to partner with and prioritize them
 Analyze partner growth and compare to revenue from that partner - continuing the work from previous efforts by interns
 Help with licensing objectives to help support R& C, Factiva and Newswires growth
 Document all collateral that exists with partners/publishers and identify the gaps
 Audit invoices/contracts and use Salesforce to update details together with billing
 Help with the onboarding of new partners/publishers, and support of existing partners/publishers
 You Have:
 Completed 3 years of University
 Intellectual curiosity and are willing to express thoughts freely
 Superb interpersonal, presentational and written skills
 Ability to process large amounts of data
 Ability to develop new approaches to complex problems and convey these solutions simply, in the form of presentations and specifications
 Well rounded personality and experiences
 Management, Product Development, I T Management, Legal, Data Analysis or related background
