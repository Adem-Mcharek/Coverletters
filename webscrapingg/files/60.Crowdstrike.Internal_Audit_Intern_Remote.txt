About The Role: We are looking for an energetic summer intern to be a part of our dynamic Internal Audit team for a 12-week period. This position will report directly to the Internal Audit Manager. This person will support projects and assigned tasks that will cultivate their talents and skillsets while providing real-world work experience as they embark on their professional career. The candidate in this role will support each function within the Internal Audit team: (1) Internal Audit Operations, (2) Sarbanes- Oxley, and (3) Enterprise Risk Management. Under the direct guidance of a Crowd Strike mentor within each Internal Audit group, you may be assigned tasks such as assisting with risk assessment activities, analyzing data, testing internal controls, and more. If you welcome a high-growth, fast-paced environment and are eager to learn, we’re excited to hear from you! Location: Option 1: Majority Remote The majority will be remote with the potential for some travel depending on your work location, i.e., if you are in the San Francisco Bay Area, we may ask that you travel to the Sunnyvale office for team meetings or functions.   Option 2: Fully Remote Fully remote and requires no travel. Your work location is the city/town/province you will be working from remotely. Interns are also invited to work out of the Crowd Strike office located closest to their remote location.
