Since 1993, N V I D I A has pioneered visual computing, the art and science of computer graphics. With our invention of the G P U - the engine of modern visual computing - the field has expanded to encompass video games, movie production, product design, medical diagnosis and scientific research. Today, visual computing is becoming increasingly central to how people interact with technology, and there has never been a more exciting time to join our team! Join the team that focuses on leading Original Equipment Manufacturers ( O E Ms) to help N V I D I A build their business! We seek a high-energy student interested in learning how N V I D I A works with O E Ms and builds solutions to sell in their marketplace. Partner with our team to establish standard processes for a joint sales motion with O E Ms and our strategic partners. What you’ll be doing: Help N V I D I A partner more effectively as you work closely with our O E M engagement managers to preform an in-depth analysis of O E M go-to-market strategy and supporting programs. Devise formal and engaging messaging frameworks that translate highly technical information into clear solutions that can be used by sellers Collaborate closely with the marketplace and N V I D I A partners to build solutions (private offers, etc.) that generate market momentum and scale N V I D I A technologies. Create sales plays with corresponding incentive programs to engage sellers to demonstrate N V I D I A solutions to broaden our market reach. What we need to see: Pursuing a B A in business, computer science, or an equivalent program General understanding of O E M’s and their offerings Experience working closely with customers or partners A passion for getting things done in a highly technical and sales environment Experience creating G T M strategies and messaging Ways to stand out from the crowd: Previous work experience in the technology industry Experience with N V I D I A’s technology relating to Artificial Intelligence ( Machine Learning/ Deep Learning).
