Job Description The Web Solutions team acts as an in-house application development team that services many verticals within Western Digital. As a Web Developer Intern, you will work as part of a team to assist senior developers in creating and maintaining enterprise-level software that enables Western Digital to streamline the development and validation of our products. This may include, but not limited to, developing and testing web applications, configuring production systems and Dev Ops systems, and documenting procedures and processes. Qualifications Pursuing a B S in Computer Science or related field and graduating between December 2024- June 2025 Knowledgeable of H T M L, C S S, Javascript, C# Basic understanding of relational databases and S Q L Prefer some experience with one of the popular web frontend frameworks Preference given to candidates who will not require relocation to the Irvine, C A areas Right to work in the U S without requiring Western Digital to commence or sponsor an immigration case to employ you at any point in the future required (for example, H-1 B or other employment-based immigration case)
