Job Description We are seeking highly motivated university students to learn and contribute hands-on with our S S D engineering teams for a Co- Op.  Current student pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science,  Computer Engineering or  Electrical Engineering,  graduating December 2022 and beyond are encouraged to apply. We are recruiting for the following areas where you can be hired into a role within the group:  Firmware Validation Engineering (4 months) - As a Firmware Validation Co- Op Engineer, you will be part of a team of growth minded problem solvers that are bringing to life world-class performance S S Ds for our Enterprise customers. In this role, you will learn and develop python based automated test scripts, work with team members to support and enhance C I test infrastructure, triage and debug complex test bugs, and develop feature test specification documents. What is the R A M P Program?  The R A M P Program is supplemental to your day-to-day internship responsibilities. In this program, you will gain deeper exposure to Western Digital’s culture and the different parts of the organization.
