About U Disc U Disc is the leading disc golf mobile app and software company. We’re building the next generation of digital scoring, statistics, and tools for disc golfers to organize and connect the sport. We are looking for a data science intern for our 2024 summer internship program. You will experience the best of both worlds—data science and disc golf—while honing your analytical skills alongside a team of dedicated and passionate disc golfing engineers. U Disc interns are members of the team. You will perform analyses, create and manage data pipelines, and develop other data-oriented tooling such as statistical models, dashboards and reports which impact hundreds of thousands of disc golfers, while learning first-hand how a growing technology company operates. About the Role Use your data science skills to build and support tools which connect disc golfers and disc golf community leaders to the data they need to grow Collaborate with the data science team as well as engineering, product, growth, and customer success teams. While you’ll be working in a remote environment, you’ll also be supported with regular brainstorming and problem-solving sessions, open lines of communication, and team feedback to guide you in creating the best final product Projects are flexible and depend on the intersection of your specific background and interests with the needs of U Disc as a company as well as the greater disc golf community U Disc is a fully remote company and this will be a remote internship About you Strong problem-solving skills: able to think through issues disc golfers and disc golf community leaders face and come up with impactful solutions Experience in Python and its suite of data science and analytics libraries, or experience in a similar suite such as R and interest in learning the aforementioned Python suite Experience combining and aggregating data sets from disparate sources Familiarity with querying databases to retrieve and manipulate data Highly motivated and excited to learn new skills, tools, and technologies Attentive to detail, be willing to go the extra mile to ensure you’re always proud of your work Ability to communicate effectively with technical and non-technical colleagues Located in the United States (work will be 100% remote) What makes you stand out Comfortable owning projects from start to finish Thrive in a growing startup environment and enjoy working on complex and undefined projects Prefer autonomous work and feel motivated by freedom to explore new ideas Knowledge of and love for all things disc golf related What it’s like working at U Disc U Disc is a fully distributed team working from all over the United States. We have flexible schedules and stay connected using the best technology for remote workplaces. We schedule meetings when we need them, but we always meet once a week to connect and learn what’s going on across the team. It’s not all about work (or disc golf), so we hold regular virtual get-togethers to share our interests, play games, and hang out as a team. Our team consists of disc golfers from all levels and interests. You’ll see us take breaks to play a round on a beautiful day or try out a new release. Some of us play competitively, some of us love to watch the pros, some of us like the impact disc golf has on the world, and all of us love building the future of disc golf technology.
