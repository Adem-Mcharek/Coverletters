Job Description Looking for an exciting opportunity to gain hands-on experience in firmware engineering? We are seeking a Firmware Engineering Intern for the Runtime Team. You’ll be responsible for developing real-time firmware code for head-disk interface spacing control and manufacturing firmware to optimize control parameters. You will also develop firmware features that increase device capacity while improving reliability performance and cost. Join our team of experienced engineers and make a real impact on the industry! Develop and debug firmware for Hard Disk Drives that uses industry-standard tools and customized processes to deliver value to our customers, as part of a local team. We are looking for an intern who is comfortable with programming languages such as  C++ and Python, and can utilize them daily in the projects assigned.
