P R O G R A M S U M M A R Y At Columbia Sportswear we offer college students a unique opportunity to join our team and help solve real business problems! Bring your passion and insight to a company that is committed to driving long- term sustainable growth this summer for our 10-week summer internship at our Headquarters in Portland, Oregon!  Interns can expect to gain valuable hands-on experience within their department while building partnerships with teams and fellow interns along the way!  Location: Portland, O R  Duration: 10 weeks Expected Date Options: May 22nd- July 30th or June 19th - August 27th A B O U T T H E P O S I T I O N As part of the Commerce & Engagement Engineering team, you will be at the forefront of reshaping how consumers interact with our brands online and in-store. Our team is where creativity meets technology to forge memorable digital experiences across every imaginable channel.  We’re a group of passionate problem-solvers, dedicated to staying ahead of the curve with the latest in digital technologies. Our work fuels the company’s direct-to-consumer growth, driving forward our mission to connect active people with their passions. H O W Y O U’ L L M A K E A D I F F E R E N C E Innovate and Enhance: You’ll contribute fresh ideas and perspectives to the development of cutting-edge digital experiences. Drive Growth: You’ll play a direct role in boosting our direct-to-consumer sales by engaging with projects that attract and retain our consumers. Technology Application: You’ll have the chance to apply the latest in digital experience management and generative A I to create impactful consumer interactions. Cross- Functional Collaboration: You’ll learn to work alongside various teams to gain insights and achieve cohesive, brand-enhancing strategies. Learning and Leadership: You’ll gain valuable industry knowledge while leading initiatives that contribute to the team’s success and the company’s digital presence. Y O U A R E Problem- Solving: Creative and critical thinking abilities to tackle challenges, find innovative solutions, and improve user experiences.  Adaptability: Ability to navigate and thrive in a fast-paced environment, adjusting to new technologies and shifting project requirements with ease. Communication: Strong verbal and written communication skills, essential for collaborating with team members and articulating ideas clearly. Y O U H A V E Digital Engineering: Knowledge of programming languages such as Java Script, Python, or Swift, and experience with digital experience platforms. Design Sensibility: Understanding of U X/ U I design principles to create intuitive and visually appealing digital interfaces. Data Analysis: Ability to use analytics tools to gather insights, measure performance, and inform design and development decisions.
