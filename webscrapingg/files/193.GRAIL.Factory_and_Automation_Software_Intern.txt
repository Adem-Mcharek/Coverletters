G R A I L is a healthcare company whose mission is to detect cancer early, when it can be cured. G R A I L is focused on alleviating the global burden of cancer by developing pioneering technology to detect and identify multiple deadly cancer types early. The company is using the power of next-generation sequencing, population-scale clinical studies, and state-of-the-art computer science and data science to enhance the scientific understanding of cancer biology, and to develop its multi-cancer early detection blood test. G R A I L is headquartered in Menlo Park, C A with locations in Washington, D. C., North Carolina, and the United Kingdom. G R A I L, L L C is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Illumina, Inc. ( N A S D A Q: I L M N). For more information, please visit www.grail.com. Grail is building the world’s largest automated blood testing lab. This lab utilizes a wide variety of off the shelf lab automation solutions, controlled and orchestrated by our custom software to ensure high uptime, high throughput, and low error rate processing of patient samples. Our team has built a scalable software solution that will be launched into production during your internship. This provides Rising Seniors or Masters level students an amazing opportunity to get hands-on experience with high throughput biotech lab processing, robotics, automation, data, and software systems. This initial launch will form the foundation of a system that must be scaled to national population screening size over the coming years as Grail continues to execute on its mission. You will expected to be onsite in Menlo Park, C A with our team 2-5 days a week during your internship. Start date is June 3, end date is Aug 16. There is a one week unpaid summer break July 1-5th.
