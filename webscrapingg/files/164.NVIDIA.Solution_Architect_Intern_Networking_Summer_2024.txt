Do you want to be part of the team that brings new Artificial Intelligence ( A I) technology to use in the field? We are looking for a networking and virtualization Solution Architect to join us focused on supporting accelerated computing workloads including Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Analytics and Cyber Security on virtualized platforms. If you are passionate about advanced networking technologies and how it can be applied to the real world, we should talk. N V I D I A is the world leader in accelerated computing and is looking for solution architects like you to design and build enterprise and cloud solutions using our newest technology. You will be supporting customers in their journey, while working to complete a special project that will be agreed upon before you join. In the process you should become familiar with our data center products for A I and H P C including the D G X deep learning supercomputer, our Networking line of A. I solutions and more. You should be comfortable working in a dynamic environment. Your ability to work independently is important, and you will rely upon your excellent communication skills during consulting engagements. At the end of your internship, after being exposed to a broad scope of our technologies, you will have insight into a role as a regular solution architect at N V I D I A. What you’ll be doing: Be responsible for the setup of experiments, tests, equipment, and otherwise facilitate evaluations that help solve customer problems using N V I D I A technologies. Establish close technical ties to the customer account, establishing personal relationships to facilitate rapid resolution of customer issues. Work closely and collaborate with the N V I D I A customer account team, other Solution Architects, and/or product engineering teams. Facilitate rapid resolution of customer issues and promote the highest levels of customer satisfaction. Work to complete a special project involving key N V I D I A technologies.
