Yext ( N Y S E: Y E X T) helps organizations build digital experiences across any channel on our open and composable platform. The Yext Digital Experience Platform collects and organizes content to deliver A I-led experiences for any organization’s customers, employees, or partners. For more than 15 years, thousands of companies worldwide have trusted Yext to create seamless customer experiences at scale across search engines, websites, mobile apps, and hundreds of other digital touchpoints. Learn more at yext.com. To get the best candidate experience, please consider applying for a maximum of 2 roles within 12 months to ensure you are not duplicating efforts.
 The Systems Engineering team works closely with software engineering teams to develop, build, test, deploy, operate, and monitor the underlying infrastructure that power the Yext cloud platform. Our responsibilities range from building, operating and securing our public cloud infrastructure to designing and building out the Open Source platforms that power the Yext software stack.
 What You’ll Do

 Develop sophisticated systems that automate the build, test, and operation of our infrastructure so that we can focus our attention on the things that matter
 Work with talented software teams to optimize performance, and increase reliability of our applications, ensuring our infrastructure doesn’t skip a beat
 Build comprehensive self service functionality that lets our software engineers move faster
 Design and build our tools for monitoring and alerting to administer and comprehend increasing numbers of services

 What You Have

 Pursuing a degree in Engineering, Computer Science, or a related technical field, graduating between December 2024 and August 2025
 An interest in designing and building systems utilizing open source software and automation
 Desire to learn how cloud infrastructure, systems and software interact in a large scale environment
 Some experience building and configuring any Linux distribution ( Ubuntu, Debian, Cent O S, etc)
1 or more years of experience with modern programing languages ( Python, Go, Rust, Java, etc)
 Knowledge of version control systems (git, github, bitbucket etc) 

 Bonus Points

 Hands on experience using A W S, G C P, Azure or similar
 Deployed and configured infrastructure using tools such as Ansible, Terraform, Docker, Kubernetes, or Nomad
 Understanding of networking protocols and practices, such as I P, T C P, U D P, H T T P and Load Balancing
