Snap Inc is a technology company. We believe the camera presents the greatest opportunity to improve the way people live and communicate. Snap contributes to human progress by empowering people to express themselves, live in the moment, learn about the world, and have fun together. The Company’s three core products are Snapchat, a visual messaging app that enhances your relationships with friends, family, and the world; Lens Studio, an augmented reality platform that powers A R across Snapchat and other services; and its A R glasses, Spectacles. We’re looking for Apprentices to join an Engineering program at Snap Inc. called Snap Up. Snap Up is a fixed term 11-month engineering rotational program created by Snap to provide career development opportunities to new graduates with limited or no technical software engineering work experience. The program will take place from August 19, 2024 and ends on June 27, 2025. The intent of the program is to develop and grow all Snap Up Apprentices so that they are performing at the next level by the end of the 11 months. Those who meet those expectations will be offered full-time employment at the next level upon completion of the program. Snap Up is designed to provide Apprentices with on-the-job training and close mentorship to improve opportunities to level up into engineering professional roles. This program requires you to be in office at least four days a week at any one of our major engineering office locations: Santa Monica, Palo Alto, or Seattle. What you’ll do: Rotate within 3 of our engineering groups every 3 months Gain exposure to various technical challenges to build upon existing knowledge Learn how to conduct code reviews and ensure exceptional code quality Learn how to build robust, lasting, and scalable products Learn how to iterate quickly without compromising quality Knowledge, Skills & Abilities: Proficient in C S fundamentals Great problem solving skills Ability to quickly analyze and contextualize information  Minimum Qualifications: New Grad with a B S/ B A or M S in a technical field such as Computer Science Grad Date between December 2023 & August 2024 Limited to no relevant software internship experience  Must be able to start on August 19, 2024 Preferred Qualifications: Excellent interpersonal, written, and verbal communication skills Possess organizational skills and a healthy respect for deadlines Self-starter and “figure it out” attitude A high level of professional integrity and confidentiality Interest in gaining exposure to various aspects of Software Engineering A passion for Snapchat and creativity!"
