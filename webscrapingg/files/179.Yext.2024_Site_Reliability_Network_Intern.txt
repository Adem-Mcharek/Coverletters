Yext ( N Y S E: Y E X T) helps organizations build digital experiences across any channel on our open and composable platform. The Yext Digital Experience Platform collects and organizes content to deliver A I-led experiences for any organization’s customers, employees, or partners. For more than 15 years, thousands of companies worldwide have trusted Yext to create seamless customer experiences at scale across search engines, websites, mobile apps, and hundreds of other digital touchpoints. Learn more at yext.com. The Network Engineering team design and maintain the underlying infrastructure that powers the Yext platform. This means adapting the best open source and commercial technologies into an infrastructure architecture that everyone at Yext can utilize to develop, support and maintain the platform. Our main charge is to develop reliable, predictable, secure infrastructure in both our colo’s and clouds, and to guide various teams on how to best utilize it.
 What You’ll Do

 Develop sophisticated systems that automate the building, testing, and operation of our infrastructure so that we can focus our attention on the things that matter
 Work with talented software teams to optimize performance, and increase reliability of our applications, ensuring our infrastructure doesn’t skip a beat
 Build comprehensive & secure self service functionalities that lets engineering teams move faster
 Design and build our tools for monitoring and alerting to administer and comprehend increasing numbers of services
 Define a set of standards that services should meet, and implement the capabilities to measure and score us on these
 Diagnose and solve problems across the application, network and infrastructure stacks in a predominantly Linux environment

 What You’ll Have

 Pursuing a degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related technical field, graduating between December 2024 and August 2025
 An interest in designing, building, testing, monitoring and automating secure distributed systems and networks
 A code-first mindset to developing and testing infrastructure
 Strong interest in platform engineering, where you take a customer and product centric approach to building internal platforms
 You enjoy sharing knowledge and working as part of a team
