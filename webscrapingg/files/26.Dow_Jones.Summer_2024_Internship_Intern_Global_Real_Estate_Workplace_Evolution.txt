Job Description: Summer 2024 Internship - Intern, Global Real Estate & Workplace Evolution
 Application Deadline: February 29, 2024
 We encourage you to submit your application as soon as you can as internship applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.
 Internship Dates: June 3, 2024 - August 9, 2024 ( You must be available to work during this period)
 About Us:
 Dow Jones is a global provider of news and business information, delivering content to consumers and organizations around the world across multiple formats, including print, digital, mobile and live events. Dow Jones has produced unrivaled quality content for more than 130 years and today has one of the world’s largest news-gathering operations globally. It is home to leading publications and products including the flagship Wall Street Journal, America’s largest newspaper by paid circulation; Barron’s, Market Watch, Mansion Global, Financial News, Investor’s Business Daily, Factiva, Dow Jones Risk & Compliance, Dow Jones Newswires, O P I S and Chemical Market Analytics. Dow Jones is a division of News Corp ( Nasdaq: N W S, N W S A; A S X: N W S, N W S L V).
 About the Role:
 In this role, you will report to the Manager of Workplace Evolution, and have the opportunity to work within the different operational functions of our team. You will spend time with each senior leader throughout our global organization, learning about the following functional areas: Real Estate, Workplace Design and Delivery, Facilities, and Workplace Evolution.
 This role is hybrid, based in our New York City office.
 You Will:
 Spend time with leaders to gain a deeper understanding of their team’s organizational structure, daily workflow, primary focuses, and function as part of the overall department
 Attend project meetings with our various teams to get a sense of our active priorities and project cadences, including stakeholder meetings with groups such as Marketing, Security, Technology, and H R
 Intake and organize project requests and develop project schedules in partnership with the Workplace Project Manager
 Assist with project delivery support tasks including: taking of meeting minutes, assisting in processing of tracking logs, and other department deliverables
 Learn to read construction drawings
 Work with receptionists and office managers to promote way-finding initiatives and standardization
 Enhance the employee experience by partnering with the Training, Culture, & Community team
 Learn how the office relocation and lease breaks/renewals processes begin from a stay versus go analysis. Understand how this analysis is conducted and partake in stakeholder meetings to learn what info gathering is required to activate next steps
 You Have:
 Proficiency in tableau or a similar system
 Proficiency in G- Suite applications
 Good understanding and interpretation of construction drawings, specifications, and shop drawings
 Punctual, deadline and detail-oriented behavior
 Project management experience running multiple projects
