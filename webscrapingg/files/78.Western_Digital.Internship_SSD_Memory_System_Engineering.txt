Job Description We are seeking a highly motivated college student to join our internship program in the summer of 2024. The candidate will participate in the development of Flash Memory Testing Platform for Western Digital’s Enterprise-grade S S D. In this role, you will work with System Design Engineers to design, develop, troubleshoot and debug a fully integrated test platform including software programs, operating systems, databases, web G U I applications, networking, and tester hardware. Current student pursuing a B S or M S in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Physics, or a related field and graduating December 2024 and beyond are encouraged to apply.
