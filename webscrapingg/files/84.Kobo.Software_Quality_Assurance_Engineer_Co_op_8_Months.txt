The Role: Rakuten Kobo Inc. is looking for a Software Quality Assurance Engineer Co-op to join our Research and Development International team as a co-op for 8 months starting in May 2024. The team is comprised of many highly talented enthusiastic individuals who ensure the services and offerings from Kobo are available to both internal and external customers. Reporting to the Team Lead, Software Engineering, the Software Quality Assurance Engineer Co-op will work with the Research and Development International team made up of developers and testers and take an active part in delivering high quality software to Rakuten Kobo’s users.   The Person: A passion for software development and delivery Excellent oral and written communication skills Have a desire to work in a team but also be self-organising enough to be able to work on a task independently The Skillset: Required: Some previous experience in and Object- Oriented Software Language such as C# or Java An understanding of T- S Q L Databases Experience with web technologies ( H T M L, Java Script, C S S) Some knowledge/experience with modern U I frameworks e.g. Angular, React Knowledge of using source control systems such as Git, T F S, or Azure Dev O P S. Knowledge of software testing and techniques: Exploratory & Automation testing, White box and black box testing, Static – Manual or Automatic (using some tool e.g. – Veracode, Sonar Qube), Understanding of Testing Pyramid, Testing framework – Selenium, Playwright, etc., C I/ C D Pipeline – Jenkins, Azure pipelines, and/or Dynamic – Unit, Integration, etc. Tools: N Unit, Postman, Visual Studio, M S Test, etc.  Nice to haves: An understanding of server-side web technologies such as Web A P I and M V C Experience using an O R M framework ( N Hibernate, Entity Framework)  The Perks: Flexible hours and remote working environment Monthly commuting allowance for hybrid employees Daily lunch credit when working in-office Dog-friendly office90 Day “ Work from Anywhere” program Free Kobo device + free weekly e-book or audiobook Weekly Kobo Tech University sessions Mentorship program Employee Purchase Program & Corporate Partner Discounts Four extended long weekends in the summer About Rakuten Kobo Inc.
