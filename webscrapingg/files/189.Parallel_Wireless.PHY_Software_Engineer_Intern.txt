People come to work at Parallel Wireless because we are building the future of telecom. They stay, because they are challenged and driven by an incredible product and team.  We take pride in our commitment to employee development, and our culture fosters an atmosphere of empowerment, trust, respect, and communication. Learn more about our mission, vision and values.   We are looking for a talented and motivated intern to join our team developing physical layer( P H Y*) software for our 4 G and 5 G products. The position is a 12 months minimum internship at our office in the centre of Bristol, where you will be working alongside a team of approximately 30 people, part of a larger (300+) international R& D team motivated by building the future of telecoms.
 What you’ll do.
 You will be involved in the development, optimization, integration, and validation of P H Y software oncutting edge C P Us.  Assist with integration and test of physical layer features on End-2- End test rigs within a lab environment  Help with the creation and improvement of test and debug tools that make up part of our software release packages.  Use wireless U E (mobile) emulators and R F signal generators and analysers.   You will be involved in the development, optimization, integration, and validation of wireless algorithms on the latest Intel / A R M C P Us.
 What you will gain.
 Experience of developing real-time embedded C/ C++ software in the Linux environment. An understanding of modern C P U instruction sets and S I M D programming. Experience of working in a modern software development environment  ( G I T, J I R A, Bitbucket etc). An understanding of wireless algorithms and digital signal processing concepts.
 What you must have.
 Strong C and/or C++ programming skills.  Experience of script language (ideally Python)  Exposure to digital signal processing or wireless software algorithms is an advantage but not a requirement.  Parallel Wireless is the leading U.
