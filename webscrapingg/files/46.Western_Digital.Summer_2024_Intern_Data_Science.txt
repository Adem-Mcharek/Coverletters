Job Description We are seeking for a data science intern to join our Digital Innovation team. As a data science intern, you will work on exciting projects that leverage machine learning, statistics, and data visualization to solve real-world problems and generate business insights. You will collaborate with data engineers, analysts, and domain experts across the company and use various tools and platforms to access, process, and analyze large-scale data sets. More specifically, your responsibilities include: Assist the team of data engineers and M L engineers with the development and optimization of our data and model pipelines, as well as data cleaning/wrangling. Conducting exploratory data analysis and modeling to find patterns in large datasets. Apply data science techniques such as supervised and unsupervised learning, natural language processing, time series modeling, optimization, etc. to address business challenges and opportunities. Communicate and present data science findings and recommendations to stakeholders and management.
